Regardless of what industry you are working in, chances are high that you have been asked to move some data from A to B and add some stuff from C - oh, and did I mention not to forget about D as well?

The typical ETL tools of choice pretend to make live easier whereas in fact most teams end up with an unmaintainable ball of mud. Thanks to scalaz-stream there is an alternative available that offers composability and encourages reuse of small components.

This talk will be both an introduction to scalaz-stream and a case-study about rearchitecting an existing ETL application.
