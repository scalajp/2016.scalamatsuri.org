Our team has been developing new web services with Play framework since this year. In this session, I will talk 'how do we manage play framework application in production environment' based on a lot of problems which we have experienced, such as an integration with Docker, database migration, monitoring.

I hope that this session will help people who want to use Scala but worry about practical operation in production environment.
